    What Are The Benefits of A Diffuser?

    Some benefits of a diffuser include: improved sleep, reduced anxiety, improved relaxation, elevated mood, and stimulated focus.

    How long does the diffuser last?

    A fully filled water tank should allow the diffuser to last up to 10 hours of use.

    What light options are available with the diffuser?

    The diffuser has two different light modes: a continuous rainbow wave and a stagnant white.

    How much fragrance oil is needed?

    1-2 drops should be sufficient for the diffuser to operate for its full use.

    Can this help hayfever/sleep/allergy/sinus problems?

    Customers have stated that using this as a diffuser has helped with many sinus issues.  Maintaining correctly humidified air is very therapeutic and reduces dust and allergens in the air.
